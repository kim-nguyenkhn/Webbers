I'm a pixel scientist.

I experiment with pixels and arrange them to make spellbinding interfaces, illustrations, and graphics. No matter how good a product or service is, a bad interface can ruin the experience. I specialize in saving that experience by helping users interact seamlessly with technology through simple but powerful designs.

My current role involves producing responsive designs (and sometimes developing them) for IoT and big data visualization, making even the most overwhelming type of data easy to work through.
