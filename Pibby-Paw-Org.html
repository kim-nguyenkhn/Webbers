Project Overview

As one of the requirements for UCSD's UX Design Certificate Program, I had to design a website for the use of either ecommerce, travel booking, or pet adoptions. Being the passionate cat-lover that I am, I went with the latter.

The required pages for the assignment include:

  • Homepage
  • Content submission
  • Resources
  • Help section
  • One internal page of my choice

The assignment took a month to complete as I was communicating with my instructor to get feedback at every step of the process.

Wireframing and Common Web Interface Patterns

The homepage takeover was implemented on Wordpress and as of February 7th 2018, it can be seen at www.engagio.com. We did some testing around with the CTA colors and ended with this final design because they were proven to increase conversions.

The next step was to introduce a fresh design for our landing pages, especially for this eBook release. The CEO and marketing team felt that the current landing pages were drawl and wanted to spruce things up. We used Marketo to build these pages, in which I used custom HTML/CSS along with some graphics I created in Illustrator.
