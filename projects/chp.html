CHP Academy Cadet T-shirt Design




POSITION

GRAPHIC DESIGNER




TOOLS & SKILLS USED

ILLUSTRATOR - PHOTOSHOP




OBJECTIVE

I was commissioned to design a t-shirt for the graduating California Highway Patrol Academy cadets. This rare opportunity only came my direction because my brother was currently enrolled as a cadet and wanted me to help him with a design submission. Once a design is submitted, his class of fellow cadets will vote for their favorite.






APPROACH

The shirt would have two designs, a small one on one side of the chest and a larger image on the back. Some of the design requirements include the California flag, the American flag, seven stars, and a black strip. On the front of the shirt, we decided to include just the image of the star badge.

A quick sketch of the design elements.


The final images were composed in Photoshop. A few weeks later, my brother brought home a few shirts for me, with my design.
